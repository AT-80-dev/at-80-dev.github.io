Bibles Plus 2022

Our Bibles Plus packs help provide holistic care to persecuted believers. In many restricted countries, persecution and poverty go hand in hand. Giving believers the word of God helps them stand firm in their faith in the midst of trials and tribulations. Providing additional essentials such as noodles, rice, cooking oil, hygiene items and blankets, is an additional blessing of practical love and care for poor families. Thanks to our supporters, VOM workers and associate ministers have distributed packages to more than 7,900 families in Pakistan, Iran, China, Philippines, India, Indonesia and Myanmar..


This year, VOM Philippines hopes to distribute Bibles Plus packs to persecuted believers in both countries and, in addition, Iraq, Syria, Sudan, and Lebanon. For just $40, you can bless a needy family with a Bibles Plus pack. The packages always include a Bible, and other essentials such as Noodles, Rice, Oil, hygiene items, mosquito nets, clothes, kitchen utensils, medical kits, and blankets.
